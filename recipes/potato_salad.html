Potato Salad Recipes

Description: Beautifully cooked Potato Salad

Ingredients

  • 5 potatos
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add Potatosand cook until tender but still firm, about 15 minutes. Drain, cool, peel,and chop.
  2. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold Water to a boil; cover, remove from heat, and eggs stand in hot water 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool,peel and chop.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, relish, garlic salt, celery salt, mustard, pepper and mayonnaise. Mix together well and refrigerate untill chilled.
